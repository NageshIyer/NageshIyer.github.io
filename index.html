About

He has published over 200 papers in National & International referred Journals with citations; over 190 papers at the National & Internal Conferences, and has published over 400 technical reports.
Prof.Iyer has shown commitment to helping scientists in cutting edge research with technology developments, and students develop their full potential in their studies. Strong philosophy of research, teaching, and knowledge of many different methods to motivate scientists, students to develop their expertise in specific areas. He has been a dedicated partner to R&D, industry & academic programs and outreach events that help promote deployment of technologies, innovative and novel research contributions, learning and support the community.

His contributions are well recognised with conferement of Fellowship by:
